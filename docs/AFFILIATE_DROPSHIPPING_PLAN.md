# Affiliate And Dropshipping Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-03

This document defines how affiliate income and dropshipping fit under Auralis Digital.

## Positioning

Affiliate marketing and dropshipping are revenue channels, not separate businesses right now.

They should remain inside Auralis Digital and should not get a new repo.

## Affiliate Strategy

Use affiliate products for recommendations Auralis wants to make but does not want to sell or fulfill directly.

Good affiliate categories:

- 3D printer filament
- 3D printer tools
- creator desk setup products
- design software
- Shopify tools
- small business tools
- useful home decor or maker supplies

Recommended starting programs:

- Amazon Associates
- Shopify Collabs
- Impact
- ShareASale

## Public Site Structure

Use a section such as:

```text
Recommended Tools
```

This section should feel like curated recommendations, not a random product feed.

## Affiliate Disclosure

Affiliate links need clear disclosure.

Use plain language near affiliate links explaining that Auralis may earn a commission if someone buys through a recommendation link.

## Dropshipping Strategy

Dropshipping should be a careful test layer.

Use one supplier first:

- Spocket
- Zendrop

Do not fill the store with unrelated products.

## Dropshipping Fit

Best-fit categories:

- creative desk accessories
- LED desk lamps
- display stands
- desk organizers
- wall art accessories
- small home decor
- giftable accessories
- creator tools

## Public Collection Names

Avoid public labels like "Dropshipping."

Use names like:

- Creative Finds
- Curated Essentials
- Studio Finds
- Maker Picks

## Guardrails

- Do not add products that do not fit the brand.
- Do not imply Auralis personally makes products that are sourced through suppliers.
- Do not use fake urgency, fake discounts, fake reviews, or fake scarcity.
- Do not scale ads until products have real signal.
- Confirm margins after product cost, shipping, platform fees, taxes, returns, and support time.

## Current Decision

Affiliate marketing and dropshipping stay under Auralis Digital.

They should support the ecommerce ecosystem without distracting from the strongest realistic focus:

```text
RJ Print + Printify + curated affiliate tools
```
