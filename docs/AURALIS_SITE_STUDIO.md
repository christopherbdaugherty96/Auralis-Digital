# Auralis Site Studio (Powered by Nova)

Status: business workflow definition

Auralis Site Studio is the production workflow used by Auralis Digital to plan, build, review, and launch client websites.

Nova is used internally as a governed coworker that helps ensure nothing is missed, every website follows a standard, and each client receives a structured, high-quality result.

---

## Core Promise

Auralis does not just build websites.

Auralis uses a structured system to:

- understand each client’s business
- recommend the right website direction
- ensure all required elements are present
- prevent incomplete or rushed launches
- deliver consistent quality across projects

Nova powers the internal workflow, but all public actions are reviewed and approved by a human.

---

## How It Works (Client View)

1. Client provides business details and goals.
2. Auralis plans the website structure and direction.
3. Auralis builds a draft website.
4. Client reviews and requests revisions.
5. Auralis finalizes and launches the site after approval.

---

## Internal Workflow (Auralis + Nova)

```text
New client
-> business profile created
-> requirements checklist loaded
-> missing information identified
-> strategy recommendations generated
-> direction selected
-> sitemap and content drafted
-> requirements checked
-> QA review
-> launch readiness check
-> approval
-> deployment
```

## Requirements System

Every website is checked against a structured set of requirements.

### Universal Requirements

Every site includes:

- business name
- services
- contact information
- call-to-action
- contact or quote form
- about section
- trust proof (reviews or credibility)
- mobile-friendly layout
- SEO basics

### Business-Specific Requirements

Each industry has additional requirements.

Examples:

- Lawn care: service area, seasonal services, quote form fields
- Detailing: packages, before/after photos, service area
- Salon: booking system, services, pricing, stylist profiles
- Restaurant: menu, hours, location, ordering or reservations

### Client-Specific Requests

Each project also tracks what the client specifically asked for.

## Why This Matters

Most website projects fail because:

- important details are missed
- forms are incomplete or broken
- no clear call-to-action
- no structured intake for leads
- inconsistent quality between projects

Auralis solves this by using a structured workflow backed by Nova.
