# Sales & Referral Program

## Sales Partner Commissions
- Basic Website: $100
- Standard Website: $250
- Professional Website: $400
- 5 completed sales: +$150 bonus
- 10 completed sales: +$500 bonus

## Referral Rewards
- $100 for each completed paid website project.

## Payment Rules
A payout happens only when:
1. Client agrees to move forward
2. Payment is received
3. Project officially begins

No payout for unpaid leads, duplicate referrals, cancellations, or verbal interest only.
