# 48-Hour Execution Sprint

Last updated: 2026-05-06

## Goal

By the end of 48 hours:

- Site is credible enough for outreach.
- Lead capture path is verified.
- 10-15 outreach messages are sent.
- At least 1 real conversation is started.

This is not a planning document. It is a checklist.

## Anti-Stall Rule

Do not confuse planning with progress. Revenue comes from conversations, not features.

Track weekly:

- prospects researched
- outreach messages sent
- follow-ups sent
- real conversations started
- proposals sent

## Hour 0-2: Site Verification

- [ ] Open site on your phone. Does it load and look right?
- [ ] Open site on desktop. Does it look credible above the fold?
- [ ] Tap "Get a Free Website Review" and confirm it reaches the contact path.
- [ ] Tap phone links and confirm they dial.
- [ ] Tap email links and confirm they open the right address.
- [ ] Submit the contact form or lead path and confirm something happens.
- [ ] Check browser tab, title, metadata, and social preview basics.
- [ ] Review service-area pages for current offer alignment.

## Hour 2-4: Lead Capture Backend

See: `docs/tech/NEXT_STEP_FORM_BACKEND.md` for detailed setup.

- [ ] Confirm whether the current contact path is form-backed or mailto-only.
- [ ] If mailto-only, replace with the chosen form backend.
- [ ] Test submission end to end.
- [ ] Confirm where submissions arrive.
- [ ] Confirm spam protection or basic filtering.

## Hour 4-8: Outreach Setup

Use:

- `leads/LEADS_ACTIVE_OUTREACH.csv`
- `leads/OUTREACH_SCRIPTS.md`
- `leads/OUTREACH_PACKET.md`

Tasks:

- [ ] Pick one local niche.
- [ ] Research qualified prospects.
- [ ] Review each demo or example before sending it.
- [ ] Personalize each message with one specific observation.
- [ ] Log each prospect before or immediately after outreach.

## Hour 8-48: Outreach Execution

- [ ] Send 10-15 initial outreach messages.
- [ ] Send follow-ups to any older warm leads.
- [ ] Reply quickly to any response.
- [ ] Move interested prospects into intake.
- [ ] Send a proposal only after scope is clear.
- [ ] Track conversations and proposals in the lead tracker.

## Rule

If a task does not help lead capture, outreach, closing, delivery, proof, or retention, defer it.
