# SPRINT 01 — LAWN & POWER LAUNCH CHECKLIST

Date: 2026-05-19
Status: Draft checklist / documentation only

## Purpose

Provide the execution checklist for the first Auralis 14-day validation sprint.

This sprint tests the operator-priority brand direction through a small Lawn & Power product drop.

No Shopify or Printify action should be performed from this checklist without manual review.

---

# Sprint Identity

## Sprint Name

SPRINT_01 — Operator Series / Lawn & Power

## Primary Audience

- lawn care workers
- power washing operators
- practical dads
- service-business side hustlers
- independent blue-collar builders

## Campaign Theme

Lawn and power operator identity.

## Goal

Validate whether operator/builder products generate stronger practical interest than the current symbolic/art products.

---

# Product Checklist

Target 5 products:

1. Yard Operator Hat
2. Built Before Sunrise Tee
3. Stripe Season Decal
4. Pressure Built Hoodie
5. Provider Mode Tumbler

For each product, confirm:
- product name
- product type
- mockup direction
- short product description
- category assignment
- price target
- Printify product fit
- Shopify collection target
- content angle

---

# Shopify Collection Checklist

- Operator Series collection
- Lawn & Power collection if needed
- Art & Symbolic remains secondary

Navigation priority:
1. Operator Series

4. Dad Utility / future
5. Art & Symbolic

---

# Mockup Checklist

Minimum mockups:
- 1 campaign banner
- 1 clean product mockup per product
- 1 lifestyle mockup per product if possible
- 1 platform-ready square image per product
- 1 vertical social image per product

Visual direction:
- rugged but clean
- lawn stripes
- early morning work light
- truck/trailer context
- tools/equipment
- practical workwear identity

Avoid:
- fake grit
- excessive skull/flame cliches
- random AI-looking imagery
- confusing sacred/cosmic visuals in the primary operator campaign

---

# Content Checklist

Minimum content for 14 days:
- 5 product posts
- 3 short-form video concepts
- 5 Pinterest pins
- 3 local-service/operator posts
- 2 dad/provider posts
- 1 campaign summary post

Content angles:
- early mornings
- clean stripes
- side-hustle pride
- service-business identity
- practical dad/provider identity
- work before comfort
