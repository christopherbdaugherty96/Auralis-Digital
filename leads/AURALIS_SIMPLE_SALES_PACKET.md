# Auralis Digital Simple Sales Packet

Status: outreach-ready sales reference

Use this as the simple prospect-facing explanation of what Auralis Digital offers. Keep it short, practical, and honest.

This is not a contract. Final scope, price, timeline, payment terms, and support terms should be confirmed in a quote, proposal, or service agreement.

---

## What Auralis Digital Does

Auralis Digital builds clean, mobile-friendly websites for local businesses that need to look credible online and make it easier for customers to call, request a quote, book, or send a message.

Best fit:

- local service businesses
- restaurants and hospitality businesses
- barbers, salons, and personal service providers
- contractors and home-service businesses
- small businesses that need a stronger first impression online

---

## Quick Qualification

Before quoting, confirm:

- what kind of business it is
- whether they already have a website
- whether Auralis built the current site
- what they want the website to help with
- whether they need a new build, rebuild, migration, or ongoing care
- whether they have photos, logo, domain, and hosting access
- whether they are ready to pay a deposit if the quote makes sense

Do not quote a third-party website refresh before reviewing the existing site and access situation.

## Core Offers

### Basic Website

Starting at $500+

A professional starter website for a real business presence.

Good fit when the client needs:

- a clear homepage
- mobile-friendly layout
- contact path
- basic service information
- simple trust-building structure

### Standard Website

Starting at $1,000+

A stronger website with more sections, better flow, and more trust-building detail.

Good fit when the client needs:

- stronger service presentation
- more content sections
- service area or location information
- better customer flow
- more polished layout and trust signals

### Website Refresh

Starting at $250

Available only for websites originally built by Auralis Digital.

Good fit when an existing Auralis-built site needs:

- updated text
- improved call-to-action flow
- mobile polish
- refreshed photos or small content changes
- minor trust/clarity improvements

Third-party websites are not eligible for the $250 refresh by default. They should be handled through an audit, rebuild, migration, or custom quote.

### Managed Hosting and Care

$250/month

A stress-free monthly option where Auralis Digital manages hosting coordination, deployment upkeep, lead-flow checks, routine small updates, and priority support.

Good fit when the client wants:

- less technical responsibility
- help keeping the site current
- small updates handled as needed
- form/contact checks
- priority support

This is not unlimited redesign or custom development. Larger redesigns, new pages, booking systems, e-commerce, advanced integrations, advertising campaigns, major SEO campaigns, or large copywriting projects are quoted separately.

## For Existing Third-Party Websites

If the client already has a website that Auralis did not build, do not promise a cheap refresh before reviewing it.

Recommended path:

1. review the existing site
2. identify platform, hosting, access, and technical risks
3. recommend audit, rebuild, migration, or custom quote

Reason:

Existing third-party websites may include unknown hosting setups, plugins, builders, access problems, broken forms, old SEO structures, or technical debt.

## How to Start a Conversation

Simple version:

> I build clean, mobile-friendly websites for local businesses. The goal is to make your business look more trusted online and make it easier for customers to call, book, or request a quote. Basic sites start at $500+, and I also offer managed hosting and care for clients who want everything handled after launch.

If they already have a site:

> If it is a site I built, I can usually refresh smaller things starting at $250. If it was built somewhere else, I would need to review it first because every platform and hosting setup is different. From there I can recommend an audit, rebuild, migration, or custom quote.

If they ask about monthly support:

> The managed hosting and care plan is $250/month. It is for clients who want the site hosted, checked, and updated without dealing with the technical side. It covers routine upkeep and small updates, while larger new work is quoted separately.
