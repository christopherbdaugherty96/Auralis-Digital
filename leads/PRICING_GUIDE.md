# Pricing Guide

## Purpose

Use clear value-based language when discussing pricing.

## Core Offers

### Website Refresh for Auralis-Built Sites - Starting at $250

Best for websites originally built by Auralis Digital that need cleaner wording, better trust, updated content, or an easier customer action path after launch.

Third-party websites are not eligible for the standard refresh offer by default. Quote those as an audit, rebuild, migration, or custom project because the technical setup, access, platform quality, and hidden issues are unknown.

### Starter Website - Starting at $500

Best for businesses that need a credible online presence with clear services and contact paths.

### Standard Website - Starting at $1,000

Best for businesses that need a stronger brand presence, multiple sections/pages, and a more polished conversion flow.

### Managed Hosting and Care Plan - $250/month

Best for clients who want stress-free website management, hosting/deployment coordination, routine upkeep, small ongoing updates, and priority support after launch.

This is not unlimited redesign, unlimited development, new page creation, e-commerce, booking systems, custom integrations, or major copywriting. Larger work is quoted separately.

### Custom Quote

For unique scopes, advanced features, larger builds, third-party website cleanup, rebuilds, migrations, or unclear technical situations.

## Rule

Sell outcomes and clarity, not just pages.
