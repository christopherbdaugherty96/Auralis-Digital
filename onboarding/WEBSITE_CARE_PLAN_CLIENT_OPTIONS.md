# Website Care Plan Client Options

Status: client-facing option guide

This document explains the care plan options that may be offered after a website build or refresh.

A care plan is optional. It is separate from the one-time website build price.

## Default Option: Client-Owned Website

Most clients should own their domain, hosting, and business accounts.

Auralis can help set things up and explain how to manage them, but the client remains the owner.

Best for:

- businesses that want control
- clients who already have hosting/domain accounts
- clients who only need occasional help

Client is responsible for:

- domain renewal
- hosting billing
- keeping account access safe
- requesting updates when needed

## Refresh Boundary

Website Refresh is only for websites originally built by Auralis Digital.

If a business has a third-party website, the next step is usually a Website Audit / Rebuild Consultation, rebuild, migration, or custom quote before refresh or care work is offered.

The audit/rebuild consultation gives the client a short written recommendation before Auralis promises cleanup, migration, support, or ongoing care for an unknown setup.

## Option 1: No Monthly Care Plan

The client receives the completed website and is responsible for future updates.

Good fit when:

- the site is simple
- updates are rare
- the client is comfortable managing accounts
- budget is limited

Future changes can still be quoted separately.

## Option 2: Basic Care Plan

A lightweight monthly plan for simple peace of mind.

May include:

- monthly website check
- contact form test
- broken link spot check
- one small update per month
- basic reminder support for hosting/domain renewals

Good fit for:

- small local businesses
- simple brochure websites
- clients who want occasional help

## Option 3: Standard Care Plan

A more active support plan for clients who expect regular updates.

May include:

- everything in Basic Care
- multiple small updates per month
- basic content refreshes
- simple monthly visibility notes
- priority response window

Good fit for:

- businesses with changing services
- restaurants, events, seasonal businesses
- local companies that want ongoing support

## Option 4: Managed Hosting and Care Plan

Suggested price: $250/month

A stress-free option where Auralis Digital manages or coordinates hosting, deployments, routine upkeep, and small website updates for the client.

This option is designed for business owners who do not want to think about website maintenance, technical updates, hosting coordination, deployment checks, or small ongoing edits.

May include:

- hosting or hosting coordination
- website deployment management
- routine website upkeep
- contact form checks
- link and basic error checks
- small content updates as needed
- phone/email/hour updates
- minor image swaps
- basic mobile and performance checks
- hosting/domain renewal reminders when the client owns the accounts
- priority support

Good fit for:

- busy local business owners
- service businesses
- clients without technical staff
- owners who do not want to manage hosting or updates
- businesses that want ongoing support instead of managing the website themselves

Important:

This is not unlimited custom development.

Larger redesigns, new pages, advanced integrations, booking systems, e-commerce, advertising management, major SEO campaigns, large copywriting projects, and third-party platform repair are quoted separately.

## What Counts as a Small Update

Examples:

- changing hours
- swapping an image
- updating a phone number
- adding a testimonial
- minor service text change
- updating a small section of copy

Not usually included as a small update:

- new page design
- e-commerce setup
- booking system setup
- major redesign
- new integrations
- large copywriting work
- third-party platform repair

## How to Explain It to Clients

Simple explanation:

A website build gets the site created and launched. A care plan keeps Auralis available after launch for support, small updates, and basic checks.

The Managed Hosting and Care Plan is the most hands-off option. Auralis handles technical upkeep, hosting/deployment coordination, routine checks, small updates, and priority support so the client can stay focused on running their business.

If a client does not want a care plan, they can still request future updates as separate quoted work.

## Internal Note

Do not pressure clients into a care plan. Offer it as an optional support path, especially when the client wants ongoing help or does not want to think about website upkeep.
