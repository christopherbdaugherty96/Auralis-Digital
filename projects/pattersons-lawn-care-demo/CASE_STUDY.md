# Patterson's Lawn Care & Snow Removal

Local Service Website Demo / Client Preview

Status: Client-review demo. Not public-launch ready until the Formspree estimate form endpoint is configured and tested.

## Summary

A local lawn care and snow removal website demo built from real business card materials, existing project assets, and confirmed service information. The goal is to give Patterson's a clearer online foundation with phone-first calls to action, service clarity, trust signals, and a future estimate request flow.

## Starting Point

Patterson's had confirmed business-card information for:

- Business name
- Phone number
- Lawn care and snow removal services
- "Call now for a free estimate" CTA
- Weekly, monthly, or as-needed service cadence
- Reasonable, experienced, reliable value props
- Navy, gold, and white visual direction

Grounded project findings also indicated weak online presence. No verified full website, optimized Google Business Profile, review base, service-area list, address, hours, or owner bio should be assumed.

## Work Shown

- Phone-first one-page local service website direction
- Lawn care and snow removal positioning
- Trust strip based on confirmed business-card language
- Service area placeholder that avoids unverified city claims
- Current project/gallery asset usage
- Mobile sticky call action
- Estimate form path prepared for Formspree
- Launch checklist and status documentation
