# RJ Print Brand Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-03

RJ Print is the in-house 3D print product line inside the Auralis Digital ecosystem.

## Role

RJ Print should focus on products Auralis can make, customize, price, and fulfill directly.

It is separate from Printify because RJ Print is in-house production while Printify is outside print-on-demand fulfillment.

## Brand Position

RJ Print should feel practical, custom, giftable, maker-driven, and product-focused.

Best-fit product language:

- custom 3D prints
- made-to-order prints
- personalized gift items
- desk and home decor
- small useful objects
- creative printed accessories

## Relationship To Auralis Digital

Auralis Digital is the parent business.

RJ Print is a product line inside that business, not a separate repo right now.

## Initial Brand Priorities

1. Define the strongest starter product categories.
2. Confirm pricing and margins.
3. Document fulfillment steps.
4. Keep product promises honest.
5. Use real photos and real examples as soon as possible.
6. Avoid scaling before production timing and quality are proven.
