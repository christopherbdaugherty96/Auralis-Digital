# RJ Print Product Categories

Last updated: 2026-06-05

This document defines starter product categories for RJ Print.

## Best Starter Categories

- koozies
- lamps
- clocks
- wallets
- custom 3D prints
- desk accessories
- display stands
- small home decor
- personalized gift items

## First Launch Lineup

RJ Print should not be promoted in homepage/navigation until it has enough real inventory to feel like a real product line. Target at least five products before public promotion.

Strong first five:

1. 12 oz Can Koozie
2. Chapstick Holder
3. Wallet / Card Holder
4. Desk Organizer
5. Custom 3D Print Request

This gives the launch collection a mix of everyday carry, utility, office/home organization, giftable items, and custom work entry.

## Strong Fit Criteria

A product is a good RJ Print candidate when it is:

- practical or giftable
- visually clear in photos or short videos
- reasonable to produce in-house
- easy to package and ship
- priced with healthy margin
- customizable without creating production chaos

## Avoid At First

- fragile products that are hard to ship
- oversized products with difficult packaging
- products with unclear material expectations
- copyrighted character or logo requests without rights
- products that take too long to make for the price
